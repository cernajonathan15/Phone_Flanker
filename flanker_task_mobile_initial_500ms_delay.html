Flanker Task

Focus on the center arrow only. Ignore the arrows around it. Respond as quickly and accurately as possible.

There will be 50 trials. Each arrow display appears briefly, for 83 ms. Congruent and incongruent trials occur equally often, and the delay between trials is jittered.

Press the spacebar or tap this screen to begin.
